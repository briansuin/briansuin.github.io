What I Do

Bachelor of Medicine, Shandong First Medical University. As a lawyer, I focus on investment and mergers & acquisitions, capital markets, private funds, overseas investment, international trade remedies, and data asset compliance.

  • Medium-term note (MTN) issuance for a financial leasing company
  • Private placement note (PPN) for two state-owned investment companies
  • Asset securitization (CMBS) for an industrial park in Shandong Province
  • Investment disputes involving Bitcoin and ZCash mining farms
  • Legal counsel for IPFS mining farm investment company
  • Legal counsel to a satellite navigation company
  • EU anti-circumvention investigation involving a Chinese biodiesel producer
  • EU, Mexican, and Thai anti-dumping cases involving aluminum-extrusion manufacturers
  • Equity incentive plan of a flow battery company
