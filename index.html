What I Do

Bachelor of Medicine, Shandong First Medical University. With a dual background in law and medicine, I have over eight years of experience in the medical industry and a working knowledge of programming languages including PHP, JavaScript, Go, and Python. Before entering the legal profession, I practiced as an ophthalmologist and cardiologist, participating in glaucoma and cataract surgeries, thrombolytic therapy for acute myocardial infarction, and pacemaker implantation procedures.

As a lawyer, I focus on investment and mergers & acquisitions, capital markets, private funds, overseas investment, international trade remedies (anti-dumping, countervailing, and anti-circumvention), and data asset compliance.

Major projects I have participated in include:

  • M&A transactions for a leading domestic vegetable group and a logistics company
  • New Third Board (NEEQ) listings for three Shandong-based companies
  • Registration of private fund managers and issuance of private fund products (five fund management firms in mainland China)
  • Corporate bond issuance for a Qingdao state-owned investment platform
  • Interbank market financing (PPN and MTN) for a leasing company
  • Asset securitization (CMBS) for an industrial park in Shandong Province
  • Legal counsel to a satellite navigation company, including tidal observation projects in the South Pacific and Atlantic Oceans
  • EU anti-circumvention investigation involving a Chinese biodiesel producer
  • EU, Mexican, and Thai anti-dumping cases involving two Chinese aluminum-extrusion manufacturers
